The authority for attendance, duty, posting, and transfer of police officers is vested in the department responsible for law enforcement. The specific policies and procedures regarding these matters can vary depending on the jurisdiction, but generally, police departments have internal protocols and guidelines for managing officer attendance, duty assignments, postings, and transfers.
police departments may have a rotating schedule for officers, while others may assign officers to specific geographic areas or units. The decision to transfer an officer to a different location or unit is typically made by higher-level officials within the department or agency. police unions may negotiate with the department on behalf of officers regarding matters related to attendance, duty, posting, and transfer. However, ultimately, the department has authority in these matters.
